Graphics Cards

Graphics cards are essential for any gamer who wants to play the latest games. Not all graphics cards will run the latest games but the ones on this site will. Here are our featured cards:

Nvidia RTX 2080 TI

Price: $1,199.00

The RTX 2080 is a perfect choice for a graphics card as this model is the
first to feature ray tracing technology bringing even better graphics to your
build. This card also features 11GB of GDDR6 memory as well making
this a perfect choice for gaming.

Nvidia RTX 2070

Price: $599.99

This graphics card is perfect for future proofing and for those who just can't
afford the RTX 2080 TI. This graphics card also features 8GB of GDDR6
memory making it a good substitute for the RTX 2080 TI.

Nvidia GTX 1080 TI

Price: $699.99

This Graphics card offers 11GB of GDDR5X memory, slightly slower than
the GDDR6 in the 2000 series models but still enough to pack a punch.

Nvidia GTX 1070

Price: $609.99

This graphics card offers 8GB of GDDR5 memory and is a good mid-range
graphics card which is perfect for 1440P gaming.
